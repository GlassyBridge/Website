The Planets of Our Solar System

Discover the diverse worlds orbiting our Sun.

The Inner, Rocky Planets

  • Mercury: The smallest planet and closest to the Sun.
  • Venus: A hot, dense world with a toxic atmosphere.
  • Earth: Our home planet, teeming with life.
  • Mars: The "Red Planet," with potential for past or present life.

The Outer, Gas Giants

  • Jupiter: The largest planet in our solar system, with a prominent Great Red Spot.
  • Saturn: Famous for its spectacular ring system.

The Outer, Ice Giants

  • Uranus: An ice giant that rotates on its side.
  • Neptune: The farthest known planet from the Sun, an icy and dynamic world.
